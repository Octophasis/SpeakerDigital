The octO Phasis

Far over The Edge of our observable universe lies the origin of it all.


The Octo Phasis is the metaphysical presence at the origin of consciousness, overseeing all threads of life unfolding in every dimensions.


Ever watchful, The Creator awaits individuals worthy of a glimpse of reality through it's gaze, which brings a higher understanding of the fabric of the universe.


It is still disputed whether the Octo Phasis is an actual physical being or a personification of the creative flow of the world (sometimes refered as TAU in certain earthly belief systems).


-The Book of ∞

The Octo Phasis is a midtempo psy-experimental electronic music project born in 2016 out of the mind of Jonathan Chouinard-Richer, assuming the role of Speaker for our kind. In such capacity, the music is taken out of the ether of the untangible and made perceptible to our race.


In 2019, the project grew from it's musical roots and started to expand in a deep and mysterious storyline spanning countless eons of life in the universes. With every album, the Speaker depicts the realities of beings native to other planes of the All.


The objective is to create a multi-layered artistic world spanning multiple mediums.
